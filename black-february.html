Black February

Black February is a term for the events which occurred during from 30 January until 25 February 2022, including the changes in Supreme Justice of the GUM, attack on MicroWiki@Discord by Abrams Wiucki-Dunswed, and MicroWiki for Justice.

Etymology

In a reference to the Black March of 2009, this period of conflict and crisis was originally named "Black January" by Sertor Valentinus. The term was soon replaced by "Black February" in retrospect due to that month actually seeing the vast majority of the community conflict.

Major events

MicroWiki for Justice

After a user was unbanned from MicroWiki, a petition was started that got 100 signatures for the rebanning of said user.[1] Abrams Wiucki-Dunswed nuked the MicroWiki@Discord server using a backdoor in a bot, banning thousands. A new MicroWiki server was created, this time focusing on the Wiki. Cameron was banned from all Wiki venues and removed as Supreme Justice of the GUM.
